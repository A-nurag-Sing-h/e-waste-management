Who We Are

The Regenerative Canvas for Electronics

EcoCycle is a pioneering platform dedicated to transforming how the world handles electronic waste. We bridge the gap between environmentally conscious individuals and certified recycling agencies, creating a seamless, transparent, and rewarding ecosystem for responsible device disposal.

Our Mission & Vision

Our goal is to eliminate electronic waste from global landfills by 2040. We envision a future where every obsolete device is viewed not as trash, but as a valuable resource to be harvested, repurposed, and reintegrated into the circular economy.

The Global Problem

Electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world. Millions of tons of toxic materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium are dumped annually, contaminating soil and groundwater. Proper e-waste management is no longer optional—it's critical for our planet's survival.

What We Do

EcoCycle provides a centralized marketplace for e-waste disposal. Whether you have an old smartphone, a broken refrigerator, or enterprise IT assets, our platform connects you with the right certified professionals who can safely recycle or repurpose your equipment.

How It Works

Users can browse an interactive directory of verified collection services tailored to their location and waste type. With a single click, you can book a pickup or drop-off appointment. Agencies receive the request, manage logistics through their dashboard, and ensure compliance.

Trust & Verification

Security and compliance are built into our DNA. Every agency on EcoCycle must undergo a rigorous, admin-led verification process. We ensure that our partners hold the necessary environmental certifications to handle toxic components responsibly and securely destroy any residual data.
